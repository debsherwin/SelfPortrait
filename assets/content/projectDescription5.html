Scenario

Agronomists and farm managers don’t have time to waste when they are scouting issues in the field. Their hands are dirty, and cell service can be unreliable. The height of the growing season is simply not the time to configure a new app—it has to work.

The Ceres app had been previously been a responsive version of the web app, which left it littered with tiny, clunky touch targets. It needed dedicated interactions for mobile to keep pace with scouts.
